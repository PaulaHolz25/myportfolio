My article on Krosse.info

In 2024, I focused extensively on gender equality at German music festivals. As part of an university course I was able to publish an article on this topic. For this I intervied Pau Hoff, who is helping to organize the yearly "Fusion"-Festival. While the "Fusion" is considered as a positive example, the "Deichbrand" and "Hurricane" festivals near Bremen have a shockingly poor attendance rate. To find out more, you can read my article!
